About Us

At MagiCampus, we believe that education should be free and accessible to everyone. Learning math can be a persistent and joyful journey with fun games and a personal companion. We strive to provide a fun and interactive math program compatible with the US Common Core State Standard. Our fun games and sheep provide children with a platform to learn math in a motivating way.
